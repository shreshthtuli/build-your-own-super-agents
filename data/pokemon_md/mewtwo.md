*Mewtwo* is a [Psychic](/type/psychic) type Pokémon introduced in Generation 1.

*Mewtwo* has two Mega Evolutions, available in Generation 6 games. They can be activated in battle when holding the Mega Stones, [Mewtwonite X](/item/mewtwonite-x) and [Mewtwonite Y](/item/mewtwonite-y) respectively.

## Evolution chart

* *Mewtwo* does not evolve.

## Mewtwo changes

* In Generation 1, *Mewtwo* has a base Special stat of 154.
* In Generations 1-4, *Mewtwo* has a base experience yield of 220.
* In Generations 5-7, *Mewtwo* has a base experience yield of 306.
* In Generation 2, *Mewtwo* has a base Friendship value of 70.
